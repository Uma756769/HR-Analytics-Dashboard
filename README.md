
# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze employee data and provide valuable insights into workforce performance, employee attrition, job satisfaction, salary trends, and retention.

The objective of this project is to help HR teams make data-driven decisions by identifying key factors affecting employee turnover and overall workforce management.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition and retention.
- Monitor employee demographics.
- Identify departments with the highest attrition.
- Analyze job satisfaction across employees.
- Compare employee distribution by gender.
- Study the impact of overtime on employee attrition.
- Provide actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel
- Kaggle HR Analytics Dataset

---

## 🧹 Data Preprocessing

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

- Removed duplicate records
- Changed data types
- Trimmed unwanted spaces
- Cleaned text values
- Removed unnecessary columns
- Filtered invalid records
- Prepared data for visualization

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Page 1 – HR Analytics Dashboard

- Total Employees KPI
- Attrition Count KPI
- Attrition Rate KPI
- Average Salary KPI
- Department-wise Attrition
- Employee Distribution by Gender
- Job Satisfaction Analysis
- Years at Company vs Attrition

### Page 2 – Employee Retention Analysis

- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Overtime
- Attrition by Education Field

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Research & Development department recorded the highest employee attrition.
- Employees working overtime showed a higher probability of leaving the company.
- Life Sciences had the highest attrition among education fields.
- Younger employees experienced comparatively higher attrition.
- The dashboard enables HR managers to identify workforce trends and improve employee retention strategies.
